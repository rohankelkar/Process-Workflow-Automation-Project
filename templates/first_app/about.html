What is Process 9 ?

Process 9 is a website designed to automate the workflow of a process
We have illustrated the functionality of a website by providing sample templates
These readily available templates include checklist and the task associated with it.
Once the work is done, the item in a checklist is marked with a tick mark.
Based on an analysis, task completion percent are displayed at a given point of time.
Additionally, you can create your own template.
